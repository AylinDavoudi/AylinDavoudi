My background goes from rocket propulsion and injector design to interference fits, regenerative cooling, ultrasound NDT, finite elements, control systems, and high-performance computing.

I’m especially into propulsion, fluid mechanics, and numerical simulation, but I also enjoy projects where I get to connect different tools instead of staying in one tiny box. Basically: give me a weird technical problem, a deadline, and enough coffee, and I’m probably interested.

Selected Projects

Real-Gas Droplet Evaporation

Master’s research project on evaporation models for high-pressure, transcritical, and supercritical rocket-engine conditions using Python, Peng-Robinson EOS, and fugacity-based corrections.

Python · Thermodynamics · Propulsion

Pintle Injector Design

Designed and optimized an RP-1/LOX pintle injector for a lander-scale propulsion system, combining analytical models, Star-CCM+ simulations, prototype testing, and performance validation.

Star-CCM+ · CFD · Rocket Injectors

Vulcain 2 Cooling Channels

Studied regenerative cooling channels for a rocket nozzle, comparing analytical calculations and CFD to improve heat transfer while keeping wall temperatures under control.

CFD · Heat Transfer · Rocket Nozzles

FSO Mirror Manipulator

Designed a precision mirror positioning system for inter-satellite optical communication, including compliant flexures, dynamic simulation, PID + feedforward control, and prototype testing.

Simulink · Control · Mechatronics

Conical Interference Fits

Internship project at CMD Gears investigating why theoretical dismounting forces were higher than experimental values, using analytical models, FEMAP simulations, and lubrication hypotheses.

FEMAP · Contact · Mechanical Design

Ultrasound NDT

Modeled ultrasonic inspection methods for aircraft maintenance, focusing on defect detection in aerospace structures using COMSOL and wave propagation analysis.

COMSOL · NDT · Aerospace Structures
