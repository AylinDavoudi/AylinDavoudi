Selected Projects

Pintle Injector Design

Propulsion · CFD · Prototype

Designed a RP-1/LOX pintle injector for lander propulsion from scratch. Built analytical models, ran CFD in Star-CCM+ to study mixing efficiency, then built and tested a physical prototype. Results matched predictions.

Supercritical Droplet Evaporation

Research · Python · Thermodynamics

Extending a Python evaporation solver to real-gas conditions using a Peng-Robinson equation of state. Models what happens inside liquid rocket injectors at high pressure, where classical physics stops working. Ongoing.

Mirror Manipulator — FSO Comms

Mechatronics · Control · SolidWorks

Precision mirror positioning for inter-satellite free-space optical communication. PID + feedforward control, compliant flexures in SolidWorks. Lowest tracking error in the cohort, under 30 µrad.

FF72 Firefighting Aircraft

Aerodynamics · OpenVSP · XFLR5

Wing configuration design and aerodynamic analysis of the FF72-Scooper. Assessed drag, stability, ground effect and CS-25 constraints. Proposed improvements achieving a 14% reduction in total drag.

Regenerative Cooling — Vulcain 2

Heat Transfer · CFD · Wind Tunnel

Designed and optimized cooling channels for a rocket nozzle to keep wall temperatures safe. Validated against wind tunnel experiments, measured heat transfer matched theory.

Mars Imaging Mission

Python · Compression · Cryptography

Applied information theory to a Mars rover link budget, implemented JPEG-style compression in Python, and exploited a one-time pad vulnerability in an LFSR cipher by XOR-ing encrypted images to recover Martian weather data.
