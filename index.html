Selected Projects

Pintle Injector Design & Optimization

Propulsion · CFD · Prototype

Designed a RP-1/LOX pintle injector for lander propulsion from scratch. Built analytical models, ran CFD in Star-CCM+ to study mixing efficiency, then built and tested a physical prototype. Results matched predictions.

Droplet Evaporation in Supercritical Conditions

Research · Python · Thermodynamics

Extending a Python evaporation solver to real-gas conditions using a Peng–Robinson equation of state. The goal: model what happens inside liquid rocket injectors at high pressure, where classical physics stops working. Ongoing.

Mirror Manipulator for Inter-Satellite FSO

Mechatronics · Control · SolidWorks

Designed a precision mirror positioning system for free-space optical communication between satellites. Implemented PID + feedforward control, designed flexures in SolidWorks, built the prototype. Achieved the lowest tracking error in the cohort — under 30 µrad.

FF72 Aerial Firefighting Aircraft

Aerodynamics · OpenVSP · XFLR5

Designed wing configuration and modeled the FF72-Scooper in OpenVSP. Assessed drag, stability, ground effect and CS-25 constraints. Proposed design improvements that reduced total drag by 14%.

Regenerative Cooling Channels – Vulcain 2

Heat Transfer · CFD · Wind Tunnel

Designed and optimized cooling channels for a rocket nozzle to keep wall temperatures in the safe zone. Validated with wind tunnel experiments comparing measured heat transfer against theory.

Mars Imaging Mission – Information Engineering

Python · Compression · Cryptography

Applied information theory to a Mars rover link budget, implemented JPEG-style compression in Python, and exploited a one-time pad vulnerability in an LFSR cipher by XOR-ing encrypted images to recover Martian weather data. Yes, really.
